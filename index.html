Instructions for this lesson:
Create a form with three fields: rank (at least two radio buttons), first name, and last name
When the form is submitted, display an appropriate greeting depending on which rank was chosen. For example:
Hello, Capt. Julie Park
Hello, Tsgt. Ramon Sanchez
Make the text a different color depending on which rank was chosen
Ensure your form is accessible! (Hint: Use |label| elements)
